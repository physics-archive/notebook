Vibrations in a Sensitive Research Space

Goal: Obtain velocity measurements (m/s-rms) in the frequency domain using one-third octave analysis. Compare these velocities against the International Standards Organization's (ISO) vibration criteria for structures.

Performed across multiple laboratory locations, including the frame of a Bluefors Dilution Refrigerator measurement system.

Motivations

A set of Vibration Criteria (VC) may be compared against to evaluate the stability of a research space. Dilution refrigeration systems can achieve temperatures as low as a few milikelvin; here, quantum phenomena emerge, and the system becomes highly susceptible to vibrations. Excessive environmental noise can affect the cooling process, and will affect the resolution of whatever scope is used to take measurements.
