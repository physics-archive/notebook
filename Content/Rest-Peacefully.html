Introduction

Trying my best not to approach this topic politically or with inappropriate morbidity, I'd like to illustrate an ugliness that lurks within our studies. The way I see it, humanity's third melennium was reached by the skins of our teeth.

Following the discovery of quantum particle mechanics, we began deepening our understanding of this new conserved (mathematical) quantity called energy -- extending it to the subatomic level, where the strong and electromagnetic forces are dominant.

For the first time in humankind, we were commanding forces that better-resemble natural disasters, rather than a gadget dreamed up by people. Of course, the nuclear age of 1940 is miniscule in comparison to the leviathan of today's.
