Energy Conservation from Newton's Eq. of Motion

$$ \begin{align} \vect{F} &= m \frac{dv}{dt} \\ &= m \frac{dv}{dx} \cancelto{v(t)}{\frac{dx}{dt}} \\ &= m \frac{dv}{dx} v(t) \\ &= m \frac{dv}{dx} \frac{d ( \frac{v^2}{2})}{dv} \\ \vec{F} &= \frac{1}{2} m \frac{d}{dx} \left[v^2\right] \end{align}$$
